Yoga
Savasana
Steps

With your feet a little wider than your hips, lie flat on your back.

Give yourself permission to spread your toes apart.

Place your arms at a 45-degree angle adjacent to your back.

Make sure your shoulders, head, and neck line up with your spine.

As you let your body relax completely, take a deep breath.

Hold this position for ten to twenty minutes.

Sukhasana
Steps

Place your feet just below your knees while sitting on the floor with your legs crossed.

Place your hands, palms up or down, on your knees or lap. Using a mudra while seated is not required.

To stretch your spine, push your hip bones into the floor and raise the crown of your head.

Lower and lower your shoulders. Next, push your chest towards the front of the room.

Calm your jaw, belly, and face.

Place your tongue exactly behind your front teeth on the roof of your mouth

Take a deep breath through your nose and into your abdomen. As long as you feel comfortable, hold your breath.
